Looking for some Catalonian culture that blends unwinding on the beach with checking out some pretty lush landscapes and sampling some of the finest seafood around? You’ll be after a holiday in Santa Susanna, then. Not too far from some of Spain’s biggest cities (a day trip to Barcelona is about an hour’s drive away), this part of the country delights in a more chilled-out way of living that still has plenty to offer holidaymakers. For a start, you could probably spend all week simply touring the beaches, wading in and out of the calming shallow water, and finding yourself a beach-side local. Alternatively, you could take to the streets to haggle it out at the various daily and weekly markets. It’s a truly traditional experience on your Santa Susanna holiday.
